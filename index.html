Coral Bleaching Standardization

A tool to standardize coral bleaching data. Follow one of the "branches" depending on how your coral bleaching data is formatted, and our tool will standardize your data, which will output the universal coral bleaching index, or Bri.

Branch A

Follow this branch if your data is organized in different "levels" or "severities" of bleaching, for which you have a count or percentage for each of these "levels" for each species you observed.
